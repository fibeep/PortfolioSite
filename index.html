Algebra 2 Teacher

During my Junior year of high-school, I was given the opportunity to be a TA in the Algebra 2 class. I started out helping students who fell behind, but later on developed my small group of 4 students. These students eventually caught up and overtook their classmates, which led to the school allowing me to become their teacher. Personalizing the curriculum to their learning styles allowed us to advance at even faster rates, leading to my students learning 20-30% more by the end of the year. For them, this meant a new transcript reading " Algebra 2 Advanced" and the ability to join the Honors Pre-Calculus class the following year.

Private Math Tutor

Throughout the last couple of years, I have tutored countless students across multiple schools worldwide. My specialty is teaching students (6th grade on) who lack basic, yet fundamental math knowledge, which prevents them from understanding higher level math. Throughout this experience, I realized that the largest hurdle these students usually have is self doubt about whether they can actually learn math or not. After the first class, which is mainly focused in rebuilding trust in themselves, the process flows smoothly.
